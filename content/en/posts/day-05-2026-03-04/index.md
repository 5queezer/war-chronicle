## What happened today

- Five days in, the running total is staggering: Iran has fired 500+ ballistic missiles and launched roughly 2,000 drones. About 40% targeted Israel; 60% targeted US positions. *([Pentagon](https://www.defense.gov/News/Transcripts/))*
- The IRGC officially confirmed it attacked Nakhchivan (Azerbaijan) — the first time Iran acknowledged cross-border strikes on an ex-Soviet state. *([IRNA](https://en.irna.ir/))*
- Trump posted: *"24/7 strikes — seabed to space and cyberspace."* No domain is off limits. *([Truth Social](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump))*
- Bitcoin has now fallen 15% since the war started; gold is up 12%. *([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/))*
- The Strait of Hormuz remains under partial blockade. Energy markets are in sustained stress.

## Intelligence & Field Reports

- US officials confirmed to the Washington Post that **Russia is providing Iran with real-time intelligence** on the positions of American warships, aircraft, and radar systems in the Middle East — making Russia an indirect participant in the conflict. China was not reported to be providing similar assistance. *([Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/))*
- The **USS Abraham Lincoln aircraft carrier reportedly withdrew** from the active theater under pressure from Iranian missiles and drones. Iran's parliament speaker Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf mocked the retreat: *"The famous bride left the battlefield at the first encounter with Iranian missiles and drones."* *([IRNA](https://en.irna.ir/))*
- The IRGC spokesman publicly **dared the US to send its Navy to escort oil tankers** through the Strait of Hormuz — a direct challenge signaling Iran's confidence in its ability to threaten naval traffic. *([IRNA](https://en.irna.ir/))*
- Iran's Ministry of Defense vowed to deploy **"more advanced weapons"** in coming days, promising "good news" for the Iranian nation. *([IRNA](https://en.irna.ir/))*

## Where things stand

Iran is burning through its missile inventory at a high rate, but it has not stopped. US and Israeli missile defenses are absorbing the strikes, but it takes resources and time. Trump's "24/7" declaration signals the US will not pause for negotiations. Financial markets are pricing in a long war.
